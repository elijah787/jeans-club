📚 How to Use Jeans Club

Complete guide for members and staff

👤

For Members

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with email or Google to get your unique JC ID and 10 welcome points.

💡 Use a real email to receive important updates and vouchers

2

Earn Points

Every 1,000 UGX spent = 1 point. Points never expire!

💡 Refer friends with your code to earn 100 bonus points

3

Create Discount Vouchers

Go to Dashboard → Enter points (min 4) → Click "Generate Discount Voucher"

💡 Each point = 50 UGX discount. Vouchers valid for 30 days

4

How to Shop

Option A: Click "🛍️ Start Shopping" and show QR to salesperson

Option B: Salesperson scans your QR to start transaction

💡 You'll receive a receipt on your phone to apply vouchers

5

Apply Vouchers & Confirm

On the receipt screen, click "Apply Voucher" then "Confirm Payment"

Show the confirmation QR to salesperson to complete purchase

💡 Points are deducted only when you create vouchers, not when you use them

🌟 Important: Your tier is based on lifetime points (total earned, not current balance). You never lose tier status when redeeming points!
👔

For Staff

1

Staff Login

Click "Staff Login" and enter your necessary credentials

💡 Enter your name when prompted

2

Start a Transaction

Click "📷 Scan Member QR Code" and scan member's QR

💡 Member must show QR from their "Start Shopping" button

3

Add Items to Cart

Use quick buttons or enter custom items with price

Items appear in cart with running total

💡 You can remove items by clicking ✖

4

Send to Member

Click "📱 Send to Member" to generate QR code

Ask member to scan with their phone to view receipt and apply vouchers

💡 This allows members to choose their discount vouchers

5

Complete the Sale

After member confirms payment, they'll show a confirmation QR

Click "💵 Scan Member Confirmation" to scan and complete

💡 Points are automatically added to member's account

6

Redeem Vouchers

In "Scan & Redeem Voucher" section:

Scan voucher QR or enter code manually

Enter your name and click "Redeem Voucher"

💡 Vouchers can only be used once and expire after 30 days

🔔 Staff Tips: Always verify member identity. Transactions can be paused and resumed. Use "Refresh Data" to see updated member info.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know my tier?
A: Your tier is displayed on your dashboard and membership card. It's based on lifetime points (total earned, not current balance).
Q: Can I lose my tier if I use points?
A: No! Your tier is based on lifetime points earned, which never decrease. You keep your tier forever once achieved.
Q: How do I create a voucher?
A: On your dashboard, enter points (minimum 4) in the "Create Discount Voucher" section, click Calculate, then Generate. Each voucher has a unique QR code.
Q: How long are vouchers valid?
A: Vouchers are valid for 30 days from creation. Expired vouchers are automatically cleaned up.
Q: Can I use multiple vouchers in one transaction?
A: No, only one voucher can be used per transaction. Choose your best discount!
Q: What if I lose my voucher QR?
A: You can always view your active vouchers in the dashboard and click "Show QR" to display them again.
Q: How do referrals work?
A: Share your referral code with friends. When they sign up and make their first purchase, you get 100 bonus points!
Q: What if a transaction is interrupted?
A: No problem! You'll see a "Resume Transaction" button on your dashboard. Click it to continue where you left off.

💰 Points System Explained

Simple & Rewarding

Earning Points: All tiers earn 1 point for every 1,000 UGX you spend.

Example for all tiers:

  • Spend 10,000 UGX = 10 points
  • Spend 50,000 UGX = 50 points
  • Spend 100,000 UGX = 100 points

Redeeming Points: Each point is worth 50 UGX in discount.

📈 Tier Progression

Your tier is automatically calculated based on your total lifetime points earned. Points never expire and tiers only go up - once you reach a tier, you never downgrade!

How to Move Up Tiers

  1. Make Purchases: Every purchase earns points at the same rate for all tiers
  2. Refer Friends: Earn 100 points for each successful referral
  3. Participate in Promotions: Seasonal events offer bonus points
  4. Be Consistent: Regular shopping accelerates tier progression

Important Notes

  • Points are permanent and never expire
  • Lifetime points determine your tier - redeeming points does not lower your tier!
  • Tiers only increase - no downgrading
  • All tiers earn at the same rate: 1 point per 1,000 UGX
  • All tiers redeem at the same rate: 1 point = 50 UGX
